Thylakoid Atomistic Model


Authors

Lana Kazem, Ngan Nguyen, Ondrej Strnad, Ivan Viola, Deng Luo.

Description

This is an SRSI[1] internship project aimed at building the atomistic model for thylakoid. The most up-to-date structural data and knowledge are first collected and then fed into the in-house modeling tool called MesoCraft. All proteins' structures are collected from the PDB database followed by post-processing in PyMol to remove the unrelated molecules when exist. The above poster was presented by Lana Kazem in the SRSI poster session.

The thylakoid model presented is a work in progress. Please address correspondence to Deng Luo.

1. Saudi Research Science Institute (SRSI) internship program: kaust.edu.sa/en/study/saudi-initiatives/talent-development-and-management
