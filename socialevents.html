Beyond Goodbyes: A Fond Farewell to Our Colleague, Ciril Bohak

Published on December 20, 2023

We are saying goodbye to our colleague, research scientist Ciril Bohak, who has successfully concluded his tenure with us. Ciril is ready to take on the role of Assistant Professor at the University of Ljubljana in his home country. Utilizing the available printing facility, we have created a memorable 3D model of Ciril along with his workplace in printed form. Additionally, a handmade portrait of him using cotton threads has been crafted. We extend our sincere congratulations and best wishes for his continued success in the academic realm and beyond.

Group Retreat (Taif)

Published on December 4, 2023

On 29-30 November 2023, the NANOVis research group visited Taif for a group retreat. In addition to research presentations and discussions, we experienced several social activities in the beautiful city of Taif. We extend our appreciation to the KAUST GA office for their invaluable guidance throughout the trip.

Magic Leap Team at KAUST

Daniel Khayat, general manager of Magic Leap (Middle East), and Roberto Mangano, COO of Fifthingenium visited NANOVis research group at KAUST and presented Magic Leap and its applications, especially for Education and Classroom Collaboration [Tuesday, November 28, 2023].
