Authors

Xinyu Liu, Dawar Khan, Omar Mena, Donggang Jia, Alexandre Kouyoumdjian, Ivan Viola

Poster Description

This poster presents LoXR, an evaluation of LLMs executed locally on XR devices. It highlights performance benchmarking across multiple platforms, addressing the practical implications of deploying large language models in immersive environments.
